* Introduction to Graph Theory
** Networks and Relationships
- Much like group theory is the mathematical language of symmetry, graph theory
  provides languages for expressing relationships
*** Applications of graph theory
- Modeling networks
- Chemistry
- Machine learning
- Routing problems
** Terminology
- A *graph* \( \mathcal{G} = (V, E) \) is a set \( V \) of *vertices* together with a set
  \( E \) of *edges*.  Two vertices are *adjacent* if they are connected by an edge.
- A *simple graph* is a graph with at most one edge between any pair of vertices
  and no loops.
